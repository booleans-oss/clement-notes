## Most Desirable Outcome (MDO)

<Callout type="info">
    **MDO** = **M**ost **D**esirable **O**utcome

    The most desirable outcome is when the customer agrees with your initial argument (<span className="highlight">best case scenario</span>).
</Callout>

## Least Acceptable Agreement (LAA)

<Callout type="info">
    **LAA** = **L**east **A**cceptable **A**greement

    The least acceptable agreement is when the customer gives a counter-offer that meets their requirements (<span className="highlight">worst case scenario</span>).
</Callout>

## Best Alternative (BATNA)

<Callout type="info">
    **BATNA** = **B**est **A**lternative **T**o a **N**egotiated **A**greement

    The best alternative to a negotiated agreement is the best outcome you can achieve if the negotiation fails (<span className="highlight">fallback scenario</span>).
</Callout>

## Zone Of Possible Agreement (ZOPA)

<Callout type="info">
    **ZOPA** = **Z**one **O**f **P**ossible **A**greement

    The zone of possible agreement is the range of outcomes that both parties can agree to (<span className="highlight">negotiation range</span>).
</Callout>
