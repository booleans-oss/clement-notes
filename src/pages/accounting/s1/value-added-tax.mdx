## What is it ?

**V**alue **A**dded **T**ax (<span className="highlight green">VAT</span>):

- <span className="highlight">SYSTEMATIC</span> tax (always applied in prices)
- borne by final customer (paid by final customer)
- VAT is <span className="highlight orange">"transparent"</span> for companies (<span className="highlight">Tax collectors</span>)
- Generally, **20%** but can be reduced for specific products (food and books = 5.5%)
